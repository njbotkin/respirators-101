The Calculations Tool is intended to help the user to conduct several calculations relevant to respirator selection:

MUC: Calculation of Maximum Use Concentration using the APF and the Exposure Limit

TWA: Calculation of the Time-Weighted Average using measured workplace concentrations across several samples over an 8- or 10-hour period

HR: Calculation of the Hazard Ratio based on TWA and Exposure Limit

Multi-Component MUC: Calculation of Multi-Component MUCs
