PURPOSE

The My Info tool gathers all the information each user needs into one place for handy reference. Some information is entered by the user on this screen. Other information is saved here from the Chemical Lookup or Respirator Selection tools.

DIRECTIONS

My Info helps you to store all the information about your respirators. Job Names are stored along with the chemical hazards and measured exposure concentrations.
